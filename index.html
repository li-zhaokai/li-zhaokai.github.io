Biography

Zhaokai Li was born in Lishui, China, in 1993. He received the B.S. degree and Ph.D. degree in electrical engineering from Zhejiang University, Hangzhou, China, in 2015 and 2020, respectively. He is currently a postdoctoral researcher in Zhejiang University. His major research interests include the analytical modelling of PMSM and iron loss analysis.
